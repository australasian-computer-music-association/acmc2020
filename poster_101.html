Kecapi III

Patrick Hartono

soundcloud web
Session: Listening Room 2 - Tuesday 2020-07-07 8am-9am AEST
Stream Link: YouTube

Details

Abstract

“Kecapi III” is the third movement of the sequential electroacoustic works that was firstly created in 2012. This work is created upon a single sound material of pre-recorded Kecapi (plucked string instrument from central java, Indonesia) manipulated and developed through granular synthesis process that created a drone-like ambient of the resonated string.

The parameters of granular synthesis are controlled through algorithms developed in Supercollider environment where each of them has specific behavior that generated irregular patterns.

Bio

Born in Makassar 1988, Patrick Gunawan Hartono is an Indonesian electroacoustic composer and creative coder. His art and musical interest are to use technology and scientific approaches as creative tools. He is also interested in 3-D sound spatialisation, analog/digital synthesis, visual music and computer game technology.

He earned a BMus in Composition (Cum laude) from Rotterdam Conservatory, Minor study at The Institute of Sonology, MMus in Sonic Arts from the University of London, Goldsmiths, and Live Electronic Course from IRCAM, Paris. In 2017 he won ICMA audience award for his generative audio-visual piece “Matrix Studies,” presented at the 43rd International Computer Music Conference.

He currently based in Melbourne, Australia for a doctoral study and actively involved in local and International computer music related communities.
